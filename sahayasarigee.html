Brief

Make a service around day to day problems you face.

Problem Statement

Lack of awareness among decision-makers and potential beneficiaries about the services offered by homeless shelters in Bengaluru is leading to underutilization of these resources, resulting in unmet needs within the homeless population.

Pain Points

  • Rapid Night Surveys which are conducted every 15 days, are not very effective in convincing the homeless to join a shelter and are unsafe for the caregivers.
  • There is a big lack of trust from the end of the homeless people. The majority are reluctant for help as they are scared and unsure.
  • There is also a huge lack of awareness from the end of the homeless people. Majority of them do not know there are services and people ready to help.

Solutions

  • To address these challenges, we propose an innovative solution: a Mobile Homeless Shelter Bus.
  • This initiative aims to bring essential services directly to homeless individuals, wherever they may be.
  • By providing a safe, mobile shelter, access to food, clothing, and healthcare, and opportunities for counseling and support, the mobile shelter bus can help bridge the gap between homeless populations and the resources they need.
